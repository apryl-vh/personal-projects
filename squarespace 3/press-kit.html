Biography

The story so far.

Short bio

Andre Bell is a seasoned professional drummer with over 15 years of experience in the music industry. Hailing from Detroit, Michigan, he has toured nationally and internationally with multiple Grammy Award-winning artists, and is currently the go-to drummer for R&B legends Ginuwine, Ashanti & El DeBarge.

Long bio

Rooted in Detroit's deep musical tradition, Andre's versatile pocket spans R&B, jazz & gospel, making him one of the most sought-after musicians for both live performances and studio recordings — including a full-length album and countless live sessions.

His stage résumé includes The Millennium Tour and an NPR Tiny Desk performance with Bow Wow, and he anchors the rhythm section for several Detroit bands and collectives. Whether behind a legend or a chart-topping newcomer, Andre brings the same thing every night: the feel.
