About Dre

A Detroit drummer with a national pulse.

Andre Bell is a seasoned professional drummer with over 15 years of experience in the music industry. Hailing from Detroit, Michigan, Andre has toured nationally and internationally with multiple Grammy Award-winning artists.

He's currently the go-to drummer for R&B legends Ginuwine, Ashanti & El DeBarge. His versatile talent — rooted in R&B, jazz & gospel — makes him sought-after for both live performances and studio recordings, including a full-length album and countless live sessions.
